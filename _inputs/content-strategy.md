# Cena Health — Brand Positioning & Content Strategy

_Seed document for Brand Analyst. Website-specific content architecture has been removed. What remains is brand positioning, audience definition, and competitive differentiation._

---

## Competitive Landscape

Key competitors and their positioning:

- **Season Health** -- Consumer-focused, emphasizes ease of access and insurance coverage
- **NourishedRx** -- B2B2C focused on health plan partnerships and cultural relevance
- **Geisinger Fresh Food Farmacy** -- Provider-integrated program with strong clinical evidence
- **Aduro** -- Corporate wellness with AI-powered personalization

## Unique Positioning

Cena Health occupies a differentiated position combining:

- Clinical integration (like Geisinger) with care coordination platform (unique)
- AI-powered personalization (like Aduro) with cultural relevance (like NourishedRx)
- Value-based care focus with multi-stakeholder approach (hospitals + payers + patients)

## Stakeholder Audiences

The brand must serve multiple audiences, each with distinct needs:

1. **Hospital systems and health plans** -- B2B sales; need clinical evidence, ROI data, integration capability
2. **Investors and strategic partners** -- need market opportunity, competitive differentiation, financial credibility
3. **Patients and families** -- need trust, program clarity, cultural relevance, accessible enrollment
4. **Clinical staff** -- need workflow integration, platform adoption support

## Key Differentiators

- Comprehensive care coordination platform, not a point solution
- Cultural competency and health equity as core design principles, not add-ons
- AI-powered personalization with clinical data integration
- Disease-specific, culturally relevant care plans
- Proven outcomes with health system partners

## Brand Positioning Statement

Healthcare infrastructure for prescription-based nutrition care. Cena Health provides the missing data, care, and revenue infrastructure for SDOH initiatives -- turning preventable health costs into improved outcomes for patients and sustainable value for health systems.

## Outcome Data (Reference)

- 30% reduction in hospitalizations
- 25% fewer ER visits
- Addresses $17B in preventable costs (market context)
