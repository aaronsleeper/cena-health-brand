Medically tailored meals (MTM) are meals designed by a registered dietitian to address the specific nutritional needs of individuals with severe or chronic illnesses.
Patients with qualifying chronic conditions (diabetes, CHF, CKD, cancer) who have been referred by their primary care provider are eligible for enrollment.
Meals are prepared in our commercial kitchen and delivered to the patient's home on a weekly schedule. Delivery windows are confirmed 24 hours in advance via SMS.
