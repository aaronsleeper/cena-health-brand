How Medically Tailored Meals Work

Each patient receives a personalized meal plan designed by registered dietitians based on their clinical diagnoses, medications, and dietary restrictions.

  • Referral from care team triggers nutrition assessment
  • RD creates tailored meal plan within 48 hours
  • Weekly deliveries of 14 meals (7 lunch + 7 dinner)
  • Outcomes tracked via EHR integration
