Density and Line Height

How density affects line height

--leading-body re-maps under data-density:

  • Compact: --leading-body: 1.45
  • Default: --leading-body: 1.55
  • Comfortable: --leading-body: 1.65

Heading and display line heights (leading-tight, leading-heading, etc.) are not affected by density tier.
